AI Agents for Business Leaders

A complete 17-part video workshop series designed for MBA graduates, executives, entrepreneurs, and professionals who want to understand and apply AI agents in real-world business settings.

1Theory & Strategy

Understanding what AI agents are, why they matter, and how they're shaping the future of work with strategic business context.

2Real-World Case Studies

Practical examples of AI agents in sales, operations, and customer success with measurable business outcomes.

3Hands-On Demos

Step-by-step n8n tutorials for building lead qualification workflows with AI, plus group activity templates.

4Compliance & Governance

Business and regulatory context, including EU AI Act compliance and risk management frameworks.

Across theory, case studies, and hands-on demos, you'll learn:

  • What AI agents are, why they matter, and how they're shaping the future of work
  • Real-world examples of AI agents in sales, operations, and customer success
  • The history of AI and today's frameworks for building agents
  • How AI agents interact with software, email, and the computer UI itself
  • The business and regulatory context, including the EU AI Act
  • Step-by-step n8n tutorials for building lead qualification workflows with AI
  • A group activity template for brainstorming AI agent applications in your company
  • Resources and next steps for implementing AI agents in your organization

Perfect for Business Leaders & Professionals

Whether you're a business student, a consultant, or a company decision-maker, this series gives you the knowledge and tools to design, prototype, and apply AI agents in practice. By the end, you'll not only understand the strategic impact of AI agents but also know how to build and demo them yourself. This series is also a blueprint for running your own workshop - after completing it, you'll be equipped to teach this workshop to others in your company, classroom, or community.
